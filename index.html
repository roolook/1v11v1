Description

1v1.lol is an online action and shooting game designed to create 1v1 battles between two players.

When you start playing 1v1.lol, you'll be put on a stage where you'll face off against another player. Your goal is to destroy the other player and win the match.

The game offers multiple game modes, including extraversion, introversion, and cooperative mode. In extroverted mode, pillars, walls, and other structures will appear to create a complex confrontational environment. In the introspective mode, there are no barriers and the player can freely move in a confined space. Cooperative mode allows you to play with a teammate for 2v2.

When entering the battle, you will be able to choose the weapons and equipment to use in the match. The game has a variety of weapons for you to choose from, including shotguns, shotguns, sniper rifles and more. You can also use different structures such as walls, stairs and guards to create strategies and advantages in battle.


But the game is not just about shooting, but also requires you to have flexible movement skills, tactical thinking and quick reaction. Along with that, the game also has a training mode that helps you hone your skills and learn the controls before engaging in the real match.

1v1.lol is an engaging and challenging game that requires the player's concentration and fighting skills. If you like shooting and want to challenge your single-player skills, then 1v1.lol is the perfect choice for you.

Control

LMB to shoot/build.
WASD to move.
Space bar to jump.
Shift to crouch.
Z, X, C, V, or Y to switch building platforms.
F, 1, or 2 to switch weapons.
R to rotate stair/reload.
G to make door on platforms.
E to open doors.
